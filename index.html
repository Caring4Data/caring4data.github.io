Overview

CARING is a platform to enhance dataset quality through open collaboration. Datasets often face challenges such as being dynamic, incomplete, and limited in perspective, making manual validation overwhelming. CARING uses a collaborative approach where users enrich datasets by adding metadata, annotations, comments, or new samples. Researchers have the opportunity to contribute to dataset improvement.
